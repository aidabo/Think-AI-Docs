# Use Cases

## Media & News Industry

### Challenges
Declining print subscriptions, falling ad revenue, youth disengagement from news.

### Solution

```mermaid
flowchart TB
    subgraph Newsroom["AI Newsroom"]
        WRITER[AI Writing Assistant<br/>Drafting / Summaries / Headlines]
        VOICE[AI Voice Distribution<br/>Article Narration / Podcasts]
        PERSONALIZE[Personalized<br/>News Delivery]
        COMMUNITY[Reader Community<br/>AI-Moderated Comments]
    end

    subgraph Results["Impact"]
        R1[⚡ 60% faster article creation]
        R2[📱 New reader demographics]
        R3[💬 Higher comment quality]
        R4[🌏 Easy multilingual distribution]
    end

    Newsroom --> Results
```

**Key Features:**
- AI writing assistant (drafts, headlines, fact-checking)
- Personalized news delivery
- AI voice narration (listen during commute/housework)
- AI comment moderation
- Page Builder for no-code breaking news pages
